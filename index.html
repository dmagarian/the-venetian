A Family Tradition

Family Owned & Operated Since 1965

In 1965, a young man named Vince Giacalone turned a dream into reality — opening a restaurant in Point Loma where people could enjoy food from his family's heritage. What began as a small neighborhood spot quickly became a San Diego institution.

Now in the hands of his sons Frank and Joe, The Venetian carries forward more than six decades of tradition. Every dish honors the recipes, warmth, and unwavering commitment to quality that Vince instilled from day one.

From the hand-tossed pizzas to the slow-simmered sauces, every plate tells the story of a family that believes good food brings people together.
